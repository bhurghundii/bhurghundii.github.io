My goal: Improve students’ grades in foreign language speaking exams by letting them practice with AI.

Who needs this?

School teachers who want their students to succeed in their class.

Why now?

  1. Too much focus on general conversation, not enough on exam practice
  2. Not enough resources to practice speaking in class
  3. Inequitable opportunity to grow

Why the British government needs to care

Language is how people communicate. Exposure to new cultures will enable better trade deals.

Why schools need to care

Learning a different language activates different parts of the brain and trains them. This actually will help students succeed in other areas of their school
