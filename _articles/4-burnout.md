I have been healing from a fantastic burnout that lasted for a few years. At some point during mid February, I found myself not feeling nearly as burned out as I used to. Not really sure what happened but things have been easier. 

Ideally, we never do this again and I am much more conscious about making sure it stays that way. 

I am much more aware of how my physical body affects my mood. I am much more aware around my screen time, my work and the way I go about my work. I am much more aware of the words I use, the thoughts I have and how it's all connected.

I have grown much more aware that I desperately [need a hobby](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Painting) that has nothing to do with a screen or other people. 

I have grown much aware how I have been sleep walking through the experience that is called existence. I intend on getting weird and bizarre. I am very good at that. I find it helps spice things up.
