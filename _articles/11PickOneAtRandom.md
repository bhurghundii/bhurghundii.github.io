This app was built because I could not for the life of me find an app which just let me select one thing from a list of things when I needed to figure what Tae Kwon Do routine to practice.
A lot of the apps could select one random thing but would either not persist the data or worse: require me to sign in for such a simple task.

How it works:

1. Hit "Add a new deck" and enter a name for the deck
2. Hit "Add a new item" and enter a name for the item
3. Add as many items as you want
4. Go back to the main screen and voila! You get one thing random,

Note: it only gives you the random things ONCE per day. This means you can't just keep hitting the button until you get what you want.

# How it works

It was very important to me that the app be as simple as possible. I wanted my items to persist so I only had to do the work once and not have touch things again.
With that in mind, I decided to forgo any real backend and just have the app store the data locally.
Turns out this was a great idea because I was able to get the app up and running in a few hours and it works great!
