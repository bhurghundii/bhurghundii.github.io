My current project requires me to not only write documentation, but also talk to the consumers of said documentation so they understand what is going on. 

The biggest thing I have learned is you have to start the documentation in the shoes of the person who is going to read it. You have to do some research on who that person is: are they using Windows? What tools do they use? What is their level of technical expertise? 

My mistake was writing it all up in the way I came to understand the system so when it came to putting the show on the road, it was almost alien to my audience.
