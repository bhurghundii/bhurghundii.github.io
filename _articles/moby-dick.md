Just finished Moby Dick. It's 90% whale facts, facts about whaling and weird stories that have nothing to do with Captain Ahab fighting a white whale. Occasionally, the story will remind you that Ahab is trying to fight a whale.

I think the book is brilliant this way. A lot of people really hate on it because it's basically just 700 pages of Ishmael having the time of his life talking about whales.

I have decided to answer those critiques over 150 years on by giving you Moby Dick (Captain Ahab's Version)

I hope you enjoy Captain Ahab's take on this adventure and it satisfies those concerns.
